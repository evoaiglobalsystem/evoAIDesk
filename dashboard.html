Pro Tips
👤
Assignment: Use "Assign to Me" in the form or bulk bar for one-click ownership. The timestamp (assignedAt) is set automatically when you assign.
🗂️
Server-side pagination: Only 15 leads are fetched per page. Use "Last" to jump to the final page, or type a page number in the "Go to" box.
⏰
Overdue filter: Click "Overdue Follow-ups" in the sidebar to instantly see all leads with past follow-up dates.
💰
Pipeline Value: Computed once on load from the first 500 active leads (excludes Won/Lost).
🔄
Board view for pipeline: Drag cards between columns to instantly update a lead's stage.
