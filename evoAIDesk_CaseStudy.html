The Company

Meet BrightSpark Digital

BrightSpark Digital is a 3-person digital marketing agency serving local small businesses — restaurants, salons, and retail shops. They run paid ad campaigns and generate a steady stream of inbound enquiries every week.

With no dedicated CRM and leads scattered across spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and email drafts, the team was flying blind — and losing deals without knowing it.

Team Size 3 people — 1 founder, 1 account manager, 1 content lead
Lead Volume ~80–120 new leads per month
Lead Sources Website forms, LinkedIn Lead Gen, referrals
Industry B2B digital marketing services
Before evoAIDesk Google Sheets + email drafts + WhatsApp (manual)
The Problem

Leads Were Slipping Through the Cracks

What their average week looked like

✕
Spend 4–6 hours per week manually copying leads from forms into spreadsheets
✕
Miss follow-ups because there was no system to track who needed to be contacted
✕
No visibility into which leads were hot vs cold — everyone got the same attention
✕
Send follow-up WhatsApps manually, one by one, when the team remembered
✕
Lose leads entirely when the account manager was on leave
"

We knew we were losing deals. We just didn't know how many or why.

— Founder, BrightSpark Digital
The Solution

evoAIDesk in 4 Steps

BrightSpark signed up for the evoAIDesk Starter plan ($39/mo) and was live in under 2 hours. Here's exactly how they used each feature:

Step 01

🔗 Unified Lead Capture

They connected their website contact form and LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms to evoAIDesk. Every new enquiry now flows automatically into one pipeline — no manual copying, no missed entries.

Website form → auto-imported and tagged by source
LinkedIn Lead Gen Form → synced directly into the pipeline

Step 02

🎯 Smart Lead Scoring

evoAIDesk's Smart Lead Scoring instantly ranked every lead as Hot, Warm, or Cold based on activity, response behaviour, and engagement signals. The team stopped wasting time on cold leads.

Hot leads flagged immediately for same-day follow-up
Warm leads put into a nurture sequence
Cold leads de-prioritised without being lost
Step 03

⚙️ Automated Follow-Ups

One click sent an intro email or triggered a WhatsApp follow-up link — pre-written, consistent, and instant. The account manager's job shifted from writing follow-ups to reviewing responses.

Intro email automated within 5 minutes of lead capture
WhatsApp follow-up link sent with one click — message opens ready to send
Proposal email template fired from pipeline view without leaving the dashboard
Step 04

🤖 DeskBot for On-the-Go

The founder used DeskBot on Telegram to check the pipeline, review leads needing follow-up, and add new contacts — all without opening the dashboard. Even while at a client meeting.

"Which leads need follow-up today?" → instant answer via Telegram
Add a referral lead on the spot, mid-conversation
No app-switching, no dashboard login required
