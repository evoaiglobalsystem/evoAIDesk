FAQ
Common questions
Can I switch plans anytime? +
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect from your next billing cycle with no penalties.
What happens when I hit my lead limit? +
You'll receive a notification before you hit the limit. You can upgrade to the next plan, add a lead storage top-up, or archive old leads to free up space.
Are all features really included in every plan? +
Yes, absolutely. AI lead scoring, automation triggers, WhatsApp outreach, email templates, activity reports — all of it is available on every plan including Starter. The only difference between plans is the number of users and leads.
Do you offer a free plan? +
We don't offer a permanent free plan, but every plan comes with a 30-day free trial — no credit card required. You get full access to everything during the trial.
Is my price locked if I sign up now? +
Yes. Early supporters are grandfathered at their launch price forever. Even if we raise prices later, you'll always pay what you signed up for.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards. Annual plans can also be paid by bank transfer — contact us for details.
