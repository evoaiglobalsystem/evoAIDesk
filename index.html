How evoAIDesk Works

1. Capture

Automatically pull leads from your website forms, LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms, and DeskBot into one unified pipeline — no manual copy-pasting required.

2. Automate

One click sends an intro email, fires a proposal, or emails a WhatsApp follow-up link — tap it and the message opens ready to send. Your team focuses on closing, not chasing.

3. Convert

Smart lead scoring instantly ranks every lead as Hot, Warm, or Cold so your team always knows who to contact first. Real-time pipeline visibility and activity reports keep everyone aligned and deals moving.

4. Stay in the Loop

Your team uses DeskBot on Telegram — check pipeline, add leads, identify leads needing follow-up — without ever opening the dashboard.

FAQ
Common questions
What does the one-time setup fee cover? +
The setup fee covers your initial onboarding: connecting your website contact forms, configuring your sales pipeline stages, setting up email/WhatsApp templates, and a walkthrough session with the team. For Team plan, it also includes data migration from your existing spreadsheet or CRM.
Can I switch plans anytime? +
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect from your next billing cycle with no penalties.
What happens when I hit my lead limit? +
You'll receive a notification before you hit the limit. You can upgrade to the next plan, add a lead storage top-up, or archive old leads to free up space.
Are all features really included in every plan? +
Yes, absolutely. Smart lead scoring, automation triggers, WhatsApp outreach, email templates, activity reports — all of it is available on every plan including Starter. The only difference between plans is the number of users and leads.
Do you offer a free plan? +
We don't offer a permanent free plan, but every plan comes with a 30-day free trial — no credit card required. You get full access to everything during the trial.
Is my price locked if I sign up now? +
Yes. Early supporters are grandfathered at their launch price forever. Even if we raise prices later, you'll always pay what you signed up for.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards. Annual plans can also be paid by bank transfer — contact us for details.
