01 Who we are

evoAIDesk is a CRM product built and operated by evoAI ("we", "us", "our") — an AI consultancy and technology company. This page explains how evoAIDesk collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal data of its users and the lead contacts they manage within the platform.

For questions about this policy, contact us at info@evoaiglobal.ai.

02 Data we collect

Account data — your name, email address, and Google account details used to sign in via Firebase Authentication.

Lead & contact data — names, email addresses, phone numbers, company names, job titles, deal values, and any notes or messages you enter for your leads. This data is provided by you and stored on your behalf.

Usage data — basic activity within the app such as page interactions, used only to operate the service. We do not run third-party advertising or behavioural tracking.

03 How we use your data

Data collected is used solely to provide and improve the evoAIDesk service — including displaying your leads, running automations, sending follow-up reminders, and maintaining your account.

We will never use your data or your leads' data for unsolicited marketing, nor share it with third parties for their own commercial purposes.
